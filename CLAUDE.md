## Product (public-safe description)
Bio-Pod is a pre-measured, biodegradable organic fertilizer pod.
Drop it in the soil, water as normal, done. 100% organic, zero synthetic chemicals,
zero plastic waste. The water-soluble shell dissolves on contact with water,
releasing a balanced organic nutrient blend directly to the root zone.
