About

Built by a scientist, not a marketing team

BioPod was founded by Natasha Syed, an environmental scientist who studies how everyday consumer products end up in our water.

Founder and CEO

Natasha Syed

Her graduate research focuses on water quality, pollutant transport, and the downstream consequences of products we use without thinking about where they go.

The insight behind BioPod came from that research. The same patterns kept appearing: convenient products with hidden environmental costs downstream. Fertilizer was one of the clearest examples. The most convenient options on the shelf were synthetic, came in plastic, required measuring that most people got wrong, and washed excess nutrients into the same waterways she was studying.

BioPod is her answer. A product that is simple enough for anyone to use correctly, made from organic inputs that belong in soil, wrapped in a shell that disappears, and designed so that overdosing is physically impossible.

Natasha Syed · Founder and CEO, BioPod LLC · Environmental scientist (MS, Environmental Resource Science; BS, Botany)

BioPod LLC is incorporated in Louisiana. The product formulation, design, and business are solely owned by the founder, with written confirmation that no institution holds any intellectual property claim.
