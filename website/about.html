Our story

Born in a lab. Built for your garden.

Bio-Pod started with a simple frustration: the products that make plant care easy too often make the planet's job harder. We set out to fix that.

NSNatasha Syed · Founder
Founder & Chief Scientist

Natasha Syed

Natasha is an environmental scientist and graduate researcher at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, where she studies water quality and how contaminants move through the environment. Her research kept surfacing the same uncomfortable pattern: products designed for convenience often carry hidden costs that show up downstream — in our soil, our rivers, and our coasts.

Bio-Pod is her response. By rethinking something as ordinary as plant food, she set out to prove that the responsible choice can also be the simplest one — no scooping, no synthetic chemicals, no plastic to throw away.

"I want feeding your plants to be the easiest, cleanest thing you do all week."

What drives us

Our values

Transparency first

We'd rather under-claim and over-deliver. We cite our sources and say what we don't yet know.

Science, not greenwashing

Every claim has to hold up. Our roots are in research, and we hold our own product to that bar.

Simplicity by design

If it isn't easy, people won't keep doing it. Sustainability has to fit real life.
