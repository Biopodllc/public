Frequently asked questions

The basics

A Bio-Pod is a pre-measured pod of 100% organic plant food wrapped in a water-soluble cellulose shell. You drop it in the soil, water as normal, and it feeds your plant as it dissolves — no scooping, mixing, or plastic.
Place one pod at the base of your plant or just under the soil surface, then water as you normally would. The shell dissolves and nutrients release into the root zone. Specific dosing per plant size will be on the product label and care card at launch.
Bio-Pod is designed as an all-purpose organic plant food — suitable for houseplants, vegetables, herbs, and flowers in pots, beds, and containers.

Ingredients & safety

Yes. Bio-Pod is made from organic, plant- and ocean-derived nutrients plus beneficial soil microbes, with no synthetic salts or chemicals. The full ingredient list will appear on the product label.
Bio-Pod uses organic ingredients rather than harsh synthetic chemicals, which makes for a gentler product around pets and kids. As with any plant food, we recommend keeping pods out of reach and following the label directions.
The shell is water-soluble cellulose — the same plant fiber found in paper. It dissolves into the soil on contact with water and leaves nothing behind. No plastic, no microplastics.

Sustainability

Only about 9% of all plastic ever made has been recycled (OECD, 2022), and many fertilizers ship in plastic or leave plastic resin in your soil. Bio-Pod removes plastic from both the product and the packaging. See our Sustainability page for sources.
Bio-Pod is designed to be plastic-free end to end. Final packaging details and disposal guidance will be confirmed on the product label at launch.
