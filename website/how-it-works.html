How it works

Feeding plants, made effortless

No scoops. No measuring cups. No leftover plastic. Just one pod, water, and time.

1

Drop a pod

Set one pre-measured Bio-Pod at the base of your plant or tuck it just under the soil. Each pod is a single, perfect dose — no guesswork, no risk of burning roots by over-feeding.

2

Water as normal

Water the way you always do. As moisture reaches the pod, the cellulose shell dissolves and the organic nutrients begin moving into the root zone — exactly where plants take them up.

3

Watch it grow

Nutrients release gradually as the pod breaks down, nourishing both your plant and the living soil. Nothing to remove, rinse, or recycle afterward.

The science, simply

Why a dissolving shell changes everything

Most plant foods force a compromise: convenient formats tend to be synthetic and wrapped in plastic, while organic options usually mean scooping, mixing, and guessing. Bio-Pod's water-soluble shell removes that compromise.

  • The shell is cellulose — the same plant fiber as paper. It dissolves on contact with water and becomes part of the soil.
  • The dose is locked in — pre-measured at the source, so every pod delivers the same balanced nutrition.
  • Release is gradual — nutrients become available as the pod breaks down, feeding plants steadily instead of all at once.
  • Microbes do the rest — beneficial organisms help unlock nutrients and build healthier soil structure over time.
