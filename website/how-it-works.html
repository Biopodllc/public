How it works

Three steps. No mess. No measuring.

BioPod is a pre-measured plant food pod made from organic inputs, wrapped in a shell that dissolves in water. One pod replaces the entire measure-mix-apply workflow. Place a pod near plant roots, cover with a thin layer of soil, and water normally. That is the entire process.

1 Drop

Place a pod

Set one pre-measured BioPod at the base of your plant or just below the soil surface. The dose is already calibrated for one indoor plant. There is nothing to scoop, weigh, dilute, or calculate. If you can set down a coin, you can feed your plant.

2 Water

Water as normal

Water the way you always do. When water reaches the pod, the cellulose shell dissolves in 30 to 90 seconds at room temperature, carrying a balanced organic nutrient blend directly to the root zone. This is fundamentally different from liquid fertilizers that must be dissolved in a watering can first. BioPod works with the soil, not in the watering can. The nutrients activate where they are needed most.

3 Grow

Watch it grow

Nutrients release gradually over 8 to 10 weeks as the pod breaks down, feeding both your plant and the living soil around it. The staged release means your plant gets steady, gentle nutrition rather than a single chemical spike followed by nothing. No residue remains in the soil. There is nothing to dig up, replace, or remove.

The feeding cycle

One pod. 8 to 10 weeks. One full year per 12-pack.

Each BioPod delivers a complete feeding cycle of 8 to 10 weeks under normal indoor conditions. A single 12-pack feeds one indoor plant for an entire year, roughly five to six pods per year, with natural variation based on plant type, pot size, and watering frequency.

What makes this different

This is not another fertilizer in a different package

Most plant food products are a single ingredient or a uniform blend delivered through one mechanism. BioPod is a multi-compartment system where different nutrient functions activate at different stages after watering.

The fast-acting minerals produce visible greening within days. The slow-release nitrogen feeds over weeks. The beneficial microbe colony establishes over months. The phosphorus supports long-term root health. No single-ingredient spike or uniform granular blend can replicate this staged delivery.

This four-compartment architecture is the basis of our patent-pending technology, protected by a provisional application filed with the United States Patent and Trademark Office. It is not a marketing distinction. It is an engineering distinction.
