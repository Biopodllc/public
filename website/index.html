The problem

Feeding your plants shouldn't cost the planet

Conventional plant food asks you to choose between convenience and conscience. Bio-Pod refuses the trade-off.

Messy guesswork

Scooping, diluting, and eyeballing doses means it's easy to over-feed, burn roots, or waste product.

Synthetic chemicals

Most fertilizers rely on synthetic salts that wash into waterways and feed the algae blooms choking our rivers and coasts.

Plastic waste

Bottles, bags, and resin-coated granules leave plastic in your soil and in the landfill long after the nutrients are gone.

One pod solves all three. 100% organic · pre-measured · water-soluble shell that simply melts into the soil.
How it works

Three steps. No mess. No measuring.

1

Drop a pod

Place one pre-measured Bio-Pod at the base of your plant or just under the soil. The dose is already perfect — nothing to scoop or weigh.

2

Water as normal

Water like you always do. The cellulose shell dissolves on contact and carries a balanced organic nutrient blend straight to the root zone.

3

Watch it grow

Nutrients release gently as the pod breaks down, feeding both your plant and the living soil around it. No residue, no leftover plastic.

What's inside

A water-soluble shell and a living nutrient blend

Bio-Pod starts with a paper-thin cellulose shell — the same plant fiber as paper — that dissolves the moment it meets water. Inside is a balanced organic blend designed to feed plants and soil alike.

Plant proteins

Slow-release nitrogen from plant meals for steady, gentle growth.

Sea kelp

Trace minerals and natural growth compounds from ocean seaweed.

Mineral potassium

For strong roots, blooms, and fruit.

Beneficial soil microbes

Living organisms that help unlock nutrients and build healthy soil.
