The problem

Why feeding indoor plants goes wrong

According to our research and conversations with indoor plant owners, the same frustrations come up again and again. These are the barriers that keep people from feeding their plants well.

Fear of killing plants

The number one barrier to plant ownership is not light or watering. It is the fear of incorrect fertilizer application. Measuring cups, chemical ratios, and dosing caps create anxiety that keeps people from feeding their plants at all.

Products built for the wrong customer

The fertilizer industry was designed for commercial farms, lawns, and outdoor gardens. Indoor plant owners in apartments, dorm rooms, and offices are being served products that assume horticultural expertise they do not have.

Plastic and synthetic runoff

Bottles, bags, and resin-coated granules leave plastic in soil and landfills. Synthetic salt fertilizers wash into waterways, fueling the algae blooms choking rivers and coasts across the Gulf region and beyond.

One pod solves all three. Made from organic inputs, pre-measured, with a water-soluble shell that simply dissolves into the soil.

How it works

Three steps. No mess. No measuring.

1 Drop

Place a pod

Set one pre-measured BioPod at the base of your plant or just below the soil. The dose is already calibrated. There is nothing to scoop, weigh, or dilute.

2 Water

Water as normal

Water the way you always do. The cellulose shell dissolves in 30 to 90 seconds on contact with water and carries a balanced nutrient blend straight to the root zone.

3 Grow

Watch it grow

Nutrients release gradually as the pod breaks down over 8 to 10 weeks, feeding both your plant and the living soil around it. No residue, no leftover plastic, nothing to remove.

Inside the pod

A water-soluble shell and a living nutrient blend

BioPod starts with a cellulose shell made from FSC-sourced wood pulp fibers. It is the same plant fiber family as paper, fully biodegradable, non-toxic, and it dissolves in soil within 30 to 90 seconds when watered, leaving zero residue.

Inside the shell is a balanced blend built on four natural pillars:

  • Plant proteins for slow-release nitrogen and steady, gentle growth
  • Sea kelp for trace minerals, natural growth compounds, and over 60 micronutrients
  • Mineral potassium for strong roots, blooms, and visible greening
  • Beneficial soil microbes that colonize the root zone, suppress root disease, and build long-term soil health

Each pillar is individually backed by published, peer-reviewed scientific literature. The combination and the multi-compartment delivery architecture are patent-pending.
