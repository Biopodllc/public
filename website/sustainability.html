Our impact

Small pod. Real impact.

Bio-Pod was built to remove three hidden costs from plant care: plastic waste, synthetic-chemical runoff, and depleted soil. Here's the case — with the data behind it.

Our response

Designed to leave nothing behind

Zero plastic, end to end

The pod's shell dissolves into the soil and the packaging is plastic-free — so there's nothing to landfill and no microplastics left in your garden.

100% organic, no synthetics

By using organic, plant- and ocean-derived nutrients instead of synthetic salts, Bio-Pod keeps harsh chemistry out of soil and waterways.

Healthier living soil

Beneficial microbes help feed the soil food web, supporting long-term fertility rather than a one-time chemical hit.

The opportunity

Shoppers want this — and will pay for it

Sustainability has moved from "nice to have" to a purchasing driver. In a 2024 global survey, 80% of consumers said they're willing to pay more for sustainably produced goods, at an average premium of roughly 10%.4 And the value lost to single-use packaging is staggering: an estimated 95% of plastic-packaging material value is lost to the economy after one short use.5

Bio-Pod sits exactly where those trends meet — an everyday product people already buy, redesigned so the sustainable choice is also the easiest one.

Our promise: if a more sustainable material or process exists for any part of Bio-Pod, we'll keep moving toward it. Transparency over greenwashing, always.
